# Fairfield County Luxury Real Estate — Editorial Calendar

## Overview

This document outlines the content strategy and publishing cadence for the Insights blog. The goal is to establish authority in the Fairfield County luxury real estate market through high-quality, locally relevant content that serves both search visibility and reader value.

## Content Categories

| Category | Slug | Focus | Target Frequency |
|----------|------|-------|------------------|
| **Market Update** | `market-update` | Quarterly/seasonal market observations, trends, data interpretation | 1-2 per quarter |
| **Community** | `community` | Town spotlights, neighborhood guides, local events, lifestyle | 2-3 per month |
| **Real Estate Tips** | `real-estate-tips` | Buyer/seller guidance, process education, decision frameworks | 1-2 per month |
| **News** | `news` | Industry developments, policy changes, regional updates | As relevant |

## Publishing Cadence

**Target**: 2-4 posts per month

- Ensures fresh content for SEO without sacrificing quality
- Allows time for research, local specificity, and editorial polish
- Seasonal peaks: Spring (market activity), Fall (school year transitions)

## Content Guidelines

### Voice & Tone
- First-person singular ("I've observed...", "In my experience...")
- Calm confidence; no hype or pressure
- Premium, editorial quality
- Locally specific (name towns, neighborhoods, landmarks)

### Compliance Guardrails
- No guaranteed outcomes or promises
- Market observations framed as trends, not predictions
- Property values discussed as historical patterns, not guarantees
- Always direct readers to consult for personalized guidance

### SEO & LLM Optimization
- Include town/neighborhood names naturally
- Clear thesis statement in opening paragraph
- Scannable structure (H2/H3 headings, short paragraphs)
- Entity mentions: "Fairfield County", "Connecticut", specific town names
